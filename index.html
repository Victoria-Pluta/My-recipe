Salade de victoire

My recipe for a fresh summer salad bowls together crisp vegetables, crunchy nuts, flavorful cheeses, and a touch of charcuterie for a healthy, satisfying meal paired with red wine

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of any lettuce (iceberg,romaine,lettuce,butterhead)
  • 1/2 cup of arugula salad
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Couple of cucumber slices
  • 1/4 cup roasted red peppers, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh figs or apple slices (optional for sweetness)
  • 1/2 cup red or green grapes, halved
  • 30g creamy brie or goat cheese, sliced
  • 40g aged cheddar or gouda, cut into cubes
  • 3–4 slices prosciutto, torn into bite-sized pieces
  • 3–4 slices salami or peppered dry sausage, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp toasted almonds or walnuts
  • 1 tbsp sunflower seeds
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp honey or fig syrup
  • 1 tsp balsamic vinegar or simple vinegar
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Layer the fresh greens at the bottom of the bowl.
  2. Arrange vegetables, fruits, cheeses, and meats in little sections on top (like a mini charcuterie board).
  3. Sprinkle nuts/seeds over everything.
  4. Drizzle the dressing just before serving.
  5. Optional: serve with a glass of red wine.
